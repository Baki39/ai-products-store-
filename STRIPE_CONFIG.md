## How Payments Work
1. Customer clicks "Pay $XX" button
2. Redirects to Stripe Payment Link (buy.stripe.com/xxx)
3. Customer enters payment info on Stripe's secure page
4. Payment processed - Money goes to Baki's Stripe account
5. Stripe sends email receipt to customer
